About me

The short version

I work where data, product, and AI meet — turning messy, high-stakes problems into systems people can trust. I recently earned my MBA from the Yale School of Management, where I co-led the Yale AI Association.

Before Yale, I spent roughly eight years as a data scientist and product manager — building fraud and anti-money-laundering systems at J.P. Morgan (formerly First Republic), forecasting demand at Apple, and automating audits at PwC. I lead from the problem, not the feature.

Off the clock, I've led Yale's largest AI conference, invested in early-stage fintech, and helped launch a rural health clinic serving 2,000+ residents in Togo.
